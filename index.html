About CiviSunshine

I'm Grace! I've enjoyed working in nonprofit and tech in my career. I know how important it is for nonprofits to keep track of their donors, have great email lists, and keep in contact. CiviCRM is a free, open-source solution to handle all these needs. I can create a whole new CiviCRM setup for you, or help you meet your goals with an existing CiviCRM instillation.
